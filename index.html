01 — Definicja

Czym jest Reported Speech?

Direct Speech — przytaczamy slowa dokladnie, w cudzyslowie.
"I am tired," she said.

Reported Speech — relacjonujemy co ktos powiedzial, bez cudzyslowu. Zmieniaja sie czasy, zaimki i wyrazenia czasu/miejsca.
She said (that) she was tired.

02 — Cofanie Czasow (Backshift)

Tabela Zmian Czasow

Gdy czasownik wprowadzajacy jest w przeszlosci (said, told, asked…), czas w zdaniu zaleznym cofa sie o jeden stopien.

Direct Speech Przyklad (Direct) Reported Speech
Present Simple "I work here." Past Simple
He said he worked there.
Present Continuous "I am working." Past Continuous
He said he was working.
Present Perfect "I have finished." Past Perfect
He said he had finished.
Present Perf. Cont. "I've been waiting." Past Perfect Cont.
He said he had been waiting.
Past Simple "I went home." Past Perfect
He said he had gone home.
Past Continuous "I was reading." Past Perf. Continuous
He said he had been reading.
will "I will call." would
He said he would call.
can "I can swim." could
He said he could swim.
may "It may rain." might
He said it might rain.
must / have to "You must leave." had to
He said I had to leave.
shall "Shall I help?" should / would
He asked if he should help.
Bez cofania czasu — jesli czasownik wprowadzajacy jest w Present (says, tells…) lub jesli mowimy o fakcie wciaz aktualnym, czas NIE cofa sie.
"The Earth orbits the Sun." → He says the Earth orbits the Sun.
Modalne bez zmian — would, could, should, might, ought to nie cofaja sie (sa juz w formie przeszlej).
"You should rest." → She said I should rest.
03 — Direct → Reported

Jak zamienic Direct na Reported?

  • 1Usun cudzyslowie i dodaj that (opcjonalne, zalecane).
  • 2Cofnij czas o jeden stopien (patrz tabela powyzej).
  • 3Zmien zaimki osobowe i dziercawcze zgodnie z kontekstem.
  • 4Zmien wyrazenia czasu i miejsca (patrz tabela nizej).
  • 5Zmien say to sb na tell sb (np. told me zamiast said to me).
Direct
"I am studying for my exam tomorrow," Anna said.
↓ Present Continuous → Past Continuous; I → she; tomorrow → the next day Reported
Anna said (that) she was studying for her exam the next day.
Direct
"We have already eaten," they said.
↓ Present Perfect → Past Perfect; we → they Reported
They said (that) they had already eaten.
Direct
"I will help you," Mark told me.
↓ will → would; I → he; you → me Reported
Mark told me (that) he would help me.
04 — Reported → Direct

Jak zamienic Reported na Direct?

  • 1Dodaj cudzyslowie i usun that.
  • 2Przesuń czas do przodu: Past Simple → Present Simple, Past Perfect → Past Simple / Present Perfect itd.
  • 3Przywroc zaimki do oryginalnego kontekstu mowiacego.
  • 4Przywroc wyrazenia czasu/miejsca (the next day → tomorrow, there → here itd.).
Reported
She told me she had been waiting for hours.
↓ Past Perfect Continuous → Present Perfect Continuous; she → I Direct
"I have been waiting for hours," she said.
Reported
He said he would call me the following day.
↓ would → will; the following day → tomorrow; me → you Direct
"I will call you tomorrow," he said.

07 — Pytania

Reported Questions

Yes/No Questions — pytania z odpowiedzia tak/nie
Uzywamy if / whether + szyk zdania twierdzacego (bez inwersji, bez do/does/did).
"Are you tired?" →
She asked if / whether I was tired.

"Did he call?" →
She asked if he had called.
Wh- Questions — pytania ze slowem pytajacym
Slowo pytajace (what, where, when, why, who, how…) zostaje, ale szyk zmienia sie na twierdzacy. Brak inwersji, brak do/does/did.
"Where do you live?" →
He asked me where I lived.

"What time will you arrive?" →
She asked what time I would arrive.
Uwaga na szyk! W reported questions NIE ma inwersji.
✗ She asked where did I live.  |  ✓ She asked where I lived.
08 — Rozkazy i Prosby

Reported Commands & Requests

Rozkazy i prosBy (imperative) zamieniamy na told / asked + sb + (not) to + infinitive.

Rozkaz (twierdzacy)
"Close the window!" he said to me.
↓ told + me + to + infinitive Reported
He told me to close the window.
Rozkaz (przeczacy)
"Don't touch that!" she said.
↓ told + sb + not to + infinitive Reported
She told me not to touch that.
Prosba (uprzejma)
"Could you please open the door?" he said.
↓ asked + sb + to + infinitive Reported
He asked me to open the door.
09 — Reporting Verbs

Czasowniki Wprowadzajace

Zamiast said/told mozna uzac bardziej precyzyjnych czasownikow:

+ that

verb + (that) + clause
admit, agree, claim, complain, deny, explain, insist, mention, promise, reply, state, suggest, warn

+ to-infinitive

verb + to + inf
agree, offer, promise, refuse, threaten
He refused to help.

+ sb + to-inf

verb + sb + to + inf
advise, ask, beg, encourage, invite, order, remind, tell, urge, warn

+ -ing

verb + -ing
admit, deny, recommend, suggest
He denied stealing it.

+ sb + prep + -ing

verb + sb + for / on + -ing
accuse sb of -ing, apologise to sb for -ing, congratulate sb on -ing, blame sb for -ing

+ wh- / if

verb + wh- / if + clause
ask, enquire, want to know, wonder
She wondered if he'd come.
10 — Wyjatki

Kiedy NIE cofamy czasu?

1. Czasownik wprowadzajacy w Present
She says she is tired. (nie: she was tired)

2. Fakt ogolnie prawdziwy / naukowy
He said the Sun is a star. (opcjonalnie bez cofania)

3. Modalne: would, could, should, might, ought to
She said she could help. (could pozostaje)

4. Sytuacja wciaz aktualna
"I love jazz." → She said she loves jazz. (jesli nadal prawdziwe)

11 — Pelne Przyklady

Zlozoone Transformacje

Direct
"I've been working here for five years and I'm very happy," Tom told his friend.
↓ Present Perfect Cont. → Past Perf. Cont.; am → was; I → he; here → there Reported
Tom told his friend that he had been working there for five years and that he was very happy.
Reported
She asked me whether I had seen that film the previous week and what I had thought of it.
↓ had seen → saw/have seen; that → this; previous week → last week; had thought → thought Direct
"Did you see this film last week? What did you think of it?" she asked me.
Direct
"Don't forget to call me tomorrow!" she reminded me.
↓ reminded + me + not to forget; tomorrow → the next day Reported
She reminded me not to forget to call her the next day.
